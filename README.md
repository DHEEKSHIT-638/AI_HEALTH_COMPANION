# AI Health Companion

AI Health Companion is an interactive **AI-powered health assistant** built using **Streamlit** and **Google Gemini API**.  
It provides personalized meal planning, food image analysis, and expert health insights based on user-specific health profiles.

---

## Features

### Personalized Meal Planning
- Generates a **7-day meal plan** (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks)
- Customized using:
  - Health goals
  - Medical conditions
  - Fitness routines
  - Food preferences
  - Dietary restrictions
- Includes:
  - Daily calorie and macronutrient breakdown
  - Shopping list organized by category
  - Meal preparation and time-saving tips
- Meal plan can be downloaded as a text file

---

### Food Image Analysis
- Upload food images (`.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`)
- AI analyzes and estimates:
  - Calories
  - Macronutrient composition
  - Health benefits
  - Dietary concerns
  - Recommended portion sizes
- Supports images with **multiple food items**

---

### Health Insights
- Ask nutrition or health-related questions
- AI provides:
  - Science-backed explanations
  - Practical health recommendations
  - Safety precautions
  - Suggested foods or supplements
  - Research references (when applicable)

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Streamlit  
- **AI Model:** Google Gemini (`gemini-2.5-flash`)  
- **Programming Language:** Python  
- **Image Processing:** Pillow (PIL)  
- **Environment Management:** python-dotenv
